Pizza

Ingredients

  1. 4 Cups of Flour
  2. 3/4 Cup of Water
  3. Pizza Sauce
  4. Mozzerella Cheese
  5. 1/2 Tablespoon of Yeast
  6. 1/2 Tablespoon of Salt
  7. 1/2 Cup of Sunflower Oil
  8. 3/4 Cup of Milk
  9. For toppings you can choose anything you like.ie pepperoni,mushrooms,olives etc

Method

1.To prepare the Crust: combine Milk, Water, Salt, Sugar, and Yeast and stir well . Allow to sit for 5 minutes until a foam is formed . In a large mixing bowl sift the flour and add Yeat and Oil and mix until a smooth dough is formed. Place in a bowl, cover it with a cloth and place in the fridge to rise .

2. Then punch down the dough and cut into 4 and form balls.

3.To prepare the Pizza: dust a clean work surface with Flour, using your hands or rolling pin roll out the dough to a 5-mm thick, place crust on a oven tray and then spread 4-5 tbs of Pizza Sauce over the crust and then top it of by putting with cheese and desired toppings.

4.Bake the pizza in preheated oven, on 240-c for 10-15 minutes .

If you have followed all the steps correctly then your Pizza should look like this.

Enjoy your amazing cheesy pizza!
