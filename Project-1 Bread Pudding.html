Pizza

Ingredients

  1. Bread
  2. Butter
  3. Apples(diced)
  4. Vanila
  5. Sugar
  6. Cream
  7. 1 Egg
  8. Milk

Method

1.Mix together the milk,egg,cream, a little sugar, maple syrup(If it is available) and vanilla,

2. Put the diced apples between the bread and add butter on top of it.

3.Heat the oven to 175 degree c.

4.Bake in the oven for about 30 minutes.

4.After it bakes let it cool for some time and then serve

If you have followed all the steps correctly then your Bread Pudding should look like this.

Enjoy your tasty Bread Pudding!
