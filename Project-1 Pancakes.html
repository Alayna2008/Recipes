Pancakes

Ingredients

  1. 1 Cup of Pancake Mix
  2. 1 Egg
  3. 1 Tablespoon of Butter
  4. 1 Cup of Milk

Method

1.Blend the ingredients with the mix

2.Melt the butter in a pan.

3.Pour 3/4 cup of the batter into the pan.

4.Cook the pancake for 3 minutes .

5.Flip the pancake and let it cook for 1 minute

6.Take a plate and place the pancake on it.If you like to add something to your pancakes like berries,honey,maple sauce,butter etc feel free to do so.

If you have followed all the steps correctly then your Pancake should look like this

Enjoy your soft fluffy pancakes!
