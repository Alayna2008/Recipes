Brownies

Ingredients

  1. Chocolate Cake Mix
  2. Eggs
  3. Oil
  4. 2 Teaspoons of Vanila Extract
  5. Chocolate Chips

Method

1.Add all the Ingredients in 1 large bowl

2.Mix it well to form a batter.

3.Pour the batter into the baking tray

4.Adjust it with a spoon and make sure it covers all the edges.

5.Bake at 175 degree c for 25 minutes.

6.Allow it to cool down for 5 minutes.

7.If you want to make your irresistibly tasty then you can add a scoop of vanilla ice cream with some chocolate sauce on top.
